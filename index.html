How It Works

  • Enter the number of green, yellow, and red bays currently in your store into the corresponding boxes. This will generate your current service percentage.
  • Enter the target service percentage in the specified box.
  • It will then display the number of bays required to reach that percentage within the current day. The target bays and bays per employee values are rounded up.
  • Enter the number of employees working service to calculate how many bays each one needs to complete to reach the target percentage, as well as the maximum number of bays those employees can service while staying within 2.5 bays per hour.

Predicting Future Negative Percentage Impacts

  • Using the number of future bays turning red on any given day, we can predict the impact that they will have on the service percentage.
  • To get the number of bays that will change from yellow to red the next day, open the list of yellow bays by clicking on the yellow wedge of the pie chart in SmartProject.
  • Enter tomorrow's date in the search box (e.g. 01-23).
  • Go to the bottom of the page where it displays the number of results (not the total); this is the number of bays that will turn red on that day.
  • These steps can also be used with the list of green bays to estimate the percentage impact of any day within the cycle, although going beyond the next day invalidates the related bay calculations.
